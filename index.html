Resources for international students

Lindsey Wilson College (LWC) has been part of the Columbia community for more than a century. The athletics department is the pride of this school and the fact that we have one of the largest enrollment in international students from all over the globe. LWC has over 120 international students per semester and to retain those numbers, we provide many services that will accommodate our international students.
